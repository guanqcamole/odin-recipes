How to make Banh Flan

Description

Banh Flan (Flan, or egg pudding) is originally a French Recipe, hence the French name we still use in our Vietnamese Language. But Flans have become crucial memories in Vietnamese children's childhoods.

What you will need:

  • Eggs
  • Milk
  • Sugar
  • Whipping Cream & Vanilla Extract
  • Water
  • Coffee

The steps:

  1. Prepare ramekins by covering them with a thin layer of butter
  2. Make caramel
  3. Whip 3 eggs and sugar together
  4. Boil milk and cream together, constantly stir until warm
  5. Pour the milk and cream mixture into the eggs and sugar, stir lightly but constantly. Add vanilla extract
  6. Strain the mixture and pour into ramekins
  7. Bake the Flan, pouring water 1/2 height of the baking tray
  8. Enjoy!
